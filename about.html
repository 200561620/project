Welcome to Sega: Igniting Entertainment and Innovation

Welcome to the dynamic realm of Sega Corporation, a trailblazing force in the world of video games and entertainment. Situated in Tokyo's vibrant Shinagawa district, our influence spans the globe, with Sega of America in Irvine, California, and Sega Europe in London, uniting gamers worldwide through captivating experiences.

Our story began in 1960 when visionary American pioneers, Martin Bromley and Richard Stewart, laid the foundation for what would become Sega Corporation. Over the years, our evolution into a subsidiary of Sega Sammy Holdings has epitomized our dedication to innovation and excellence. From our modest inception as Sega Enterprises, Ltd. in 1965, we paved the way for coin-operated games, introducing timeless classics like Periscope.

The 1980s witnessed our revolution in gaming, as consoles like the SG-1000 and Master System redefined interactive entertainment. The debut of Sonic the Hedgehog in 1991 propelled us onto the global stage, rivaling even the illustrious Super Nintendo Entertainment System. The challenges posed by the 32X, Saturn, and Dreamcast in the late '90s only fueled our metamorphosis into a dynamic third-party developer by 2001.

A pivotal moment in 2004 saw our acquisition by Sammy Corporation, ushering in an era of transformation. The culmination of this journey occurred in 2020 when Sega Games and Sega Interactive merged under our iconic name, Sega Corporation.

Our portfolio boasts illustrious franchises such as Sonic the Hedgehog, Total War, and Yakuza. From the immersive world of consoles to the captivating realm of arcades, Virtua Fighter and Initial D Arcade Stage exemplify our commitment to pushing the boundaries of entertainment.

Immerse yourself in our dynamic history, explore ventures like amusement arcades and Sega Toys, and embrace our ongoing odyssey of innovation. As Sega shapes the future of entertainment, we invite you to join us in experiencing the pinnacle of excellence, the thrill of excitement, and the enchantment of gaming magic that spans the globe.
